# War Horde Wide Pressure Roster

## Purpose

This is the `War Horde Wide Pressure` list from the revised triple-Lokhust Necron study.

It is the best non-`Green Tide` Ork build I found for that matchup, and it is the one I’d suggest if you do not own enough Boyz for the full `Green Tide` plan.

## How I’d Organize It

- `Ghaz + 5 Meganobz` in the `Battlewagon`
- `Warboss + 20 Boyz` as the main central OC brick
- `Painboy + 20 Boyz` as the second big pressure/scoring brick
- `Beastboss + 10 Beast Snagga Boyz` in the `Trukk`
- second `10 Beast Snagga Boyz` as a side-trade unit
- `10 Boyz` as backfield or near-midfield utility
- `2x Kommandos`, `3x Stormboyz`, and `2x Gretchin` for mission pressure

## Why This Version Worked

- It keeps one real premium hammer in `Ghaz + Meganobz`.
- It has enough bodies that the Necrons cannot win just by cracking one delivery lane.
- It gives the `Lokhust Heavy Destroyers` fewer high-value targets than the more elite `War Horde` builds.
- It still plays a familiar Ork pressure game instead of going all the way into `Green Tide`.

## What It Gives Up Compared To Green Tide

- less total OC flood
- fewer giant body bricks
- slightly worse ability to absorb going second

## What It Keeps Compared To Green Tide

- more concentrated punch
- cleaner use of `War Horde`
- more comfortable transition if you already own the elite/transport pieces

## Matchup Verdict

Into the revised triple-Lokhust Necron list, this was the second-best Ork archetype I tested.

- Record in the broader study: `6-6`
- Average score: `74.6 - 74.4`

So it is not clearly favored, but it is live and much closer to even than the heavier elite `War Horde` builds.
